RELENTLESS

Scaled relational + civic education + high-opportunity voters

We invest in recruiting hard-to-reach and infrequent voters; pay them to attend a civic training; then engage them as Voting Ambassadors to mobilize their communities to vote.

Recruit

▾
  • Multi-channel recruitment: SMS, paid media, on-the-ground & digital partnerships, and referrals
  • Retention and referrals: Over 40% of 2022 participants returned in 2024 — and brought friends. Referrals were our top source of applicants (37%) in 2024.
  • Applicant vetting: Applicants are matched to the voter file (83% auto-match rate using Google Maps API for address accuracy) and scored in our 5-tier scoring system based on vote propensity, partisanship, location, referral status, phone and email verification, and fraud score. Applications are automatically accepted, rejected, or flagged for manual review based on tier.
  • Recruitment costs decrease 20% year-over-year in the same state.
  • Early funding can reduce recruitment costs by as much as 40%, by generating low-cost compounding referral chains in a less saturated media environment.

Onboard & Train

▾

Live, race-specific Zoom training sessions run by our specialized Events team. This hour of attention we get from low-propensity voters is the foundation of our program.

  • End-to-end integration: Rally’s native events platform handles event signup, reminders, join links, and automatic attendance tracking.
  • Longform persuasion training videos: We create high-quality persuasion videos focused on laying the civics foundation (What does a House representative do? How does it affect me?) and concretely imparting the tangible impacts of the upcoming election on participants’ lives.
  • Rally orientation: Participants get started by making their own voting plans, then get to work adding contacts, all with support from our team.
  • Schedule next event: In 2024, 80% of attendees scheduled their next event (the “Turnout Palooza” work session) live on the call.
  • Scale: In 2024, 41,264 unique participants attended an onboarding.

Map the Network

▾

Participants add their personal networks to Rally. Every contact is hand-matched to the voter file by a real-life associate.

  • 90% voter file match rate; 83% match uniqueness rate in 2024
  • Average network size: 45 contacts per completed participant
  • Scale: In 2024, participants mapped 1,219,658 total contacts → 1,014,902 unique voters
  • Support IDs: 1,640,940 support IDs collected across two passes in 2024
  • Relationships cross district boundaries: In 2024, over half the contacts added in a given competitive congressional district were added by contacts living in other parts of the state.

Make Vote Plans

▾

Participants make and share customized vote plans for each contact (and themselves!) with 50-state polling place data courtesy of IWillVote.

  • Polling places: The DNC’s IWillVote polling place API delivers early vote locations, dropboxes, and Election Day polling places for every user and contact
  • Plan components: Exact polling location, state-specific ID requirements, vote method (early/vote by mail/Election Day), date and time of day, transportation
  • Shareable links: Contacts view plans without needing a Rally account
  • Relational Slates: Campaigns are loaded with customizable district-specific candidate info, creating a handy ballot guide for contacts within the shared voting plan

Chase & Support

▾

Participants are chased daily via SMS, phone call, and/or email to complete their next most important action. Support is always available via our 12-hour live textline.

  • Chase = Daily nudges, personalized based on next highest priority action
  • Operational chase: Daily automated chase pushing participants to complete their next required action. Participants are automatically sorted into “chase buckets” based on progress through the program (Needs to start adding contacts, Vote plans halfway complete, Needs to schedule palooza, etc)
  • Programmatic chase: Voting-related chase messages sent around voting deadlines, participant vote status, contact vote status (Plans to vote today, 3+ contacts missed planned vote date, Early voting starts tomorrow, etc)
  • 3-phase messaging escalation: Phase 1 (within 24 hrs), Phase 2 (every other day with adjusted copy), Phase 3 (weekly). Final week: daily.
  • One ask per day rule: Never more than one operational + one programmatic message daily (relaxed final week only)
  • 12-hour daily support textline: 11am-11pm ET. Tech support, contact brainstorming, voting questions, encouragement
  • Voter assistance & protection: State-specific Voter Protection captains handle voting-related questions and issues.
  • Voter Education resource site: 50,000+ unique visitors in 2024. Issue-specific talking points, articles and social media content to share, conversation guides for: persuading third-party voters, talking to undecided, encouraging non-voters, etc
12-hour support 1 ask/day rule 50K+ resource visits

Get Out the Vote

▾

Absentee vote/early vote tracking via Catalist, daily personalized reports, programmatic chase, Turnout Paloozas and Election Day events. Every participant sees their impact immediately.

  • Daily AVEV data pipeline: Real-time vote status from Catalist for every contact, reported in Rally, in chase messages, and Early Vote Reports
  • Daily personalized “Early Vote Reports”: Emails sent to participants daily during the early voting period dividing their contacts into three lists: Already voted (celebrate), Has plan, still needs to vote (remind), Needs a plan (help). Early Vote Reports were highly popular in 2024, with a 55.7% average open rate and 15.6% click-through.
  • Election Day Vote Report: On Election Day, we send a modified email report that divides remaining contacts into buckets by planned voting time (morning/afternoon/evening voters). This saw a 65% open rate & 27.5% click-through rate in 2024. Participants were also sent SMS reminders of which of their contacts planned to vote in the next few hours periodically throughout Election Day.
  • Election Day Polls Parties: Optional 1-hour live events running every hour polls are open. Participants call through their contacts, ask voting questions, share their voting stories and stickers, and have the opportunity to win prizes. In 2024, 21,603 participants attended.

Quality Assurance

Because we pay people to organize, we build systems to ensure every dollar drives real voter contact.

All voter contact has some amount of fraud. Our approach is to detect it early, monitor it continuously, and remove bad actors before they get paid. We layer automated checks at every stage of the program.

🔍
Application Vetting
Automated checks before anyone enters the program
▾
  • Email & phone validation: Every applicant's email and mobile phone are checked for validity at signup. Invalid contact info is flagged immediately.
  • Domestic phone verification: Phone numbers without a US country code are flagged as high risk and marked ineligible.
  • IP address analysis: We record the IP address used to submit each application. Applications sharing an IP with 1-2 others require manager review; 3+ shared IPs are flagged as likely fraudulent.
  • IP geolocation: Applications originating from outside the US are automatically marked ineligible. Out-of-state IPs are flagged for review.
  • Email domain reputation: We check every email domain against the WhoisXML API for offshore registration, malware blocklists, phishing blocklists, and spam blocklists. Flagged domains receive a red flag.
  • AI text detection: Free-response answers are screened for AI-generated content. Scores above 90 trigger manager review.
  • Sanctions/AML screening: Applicant names and addresses are run against sanctions lists. Any matches are flagged for manager review.
  • Referral network analysis: If more than 35% of a user's referrals are high-risk, the referrer is also flagged as high risk.
⚠️
Fraud Grading System
Every applicant receives a composite fraud grade, updated daily
▾
  • Daily scoring: A Fraud Dashboard computes and updates fraud grades for every applicant and active participant on a daily basis.
  • Multi-signal composite: Grades are based on 10+ individual checks across user quality (email, phone, IP, domain reputation, referral quality) and output quality (event attendance time, contact uniqueness).
  • Three-tier grading: Each check produces an IDEAL, YELLOW FLAG, or RED FLAG result. Any yellow flag lowers the applicant's tier. A single red flag makes them ineligible.
  • Tiering integration: Fraud grades feed directly into the hiring tier system. Three or more yellow flags push applicants to the lowest tier. Red flags block acceptance entirely.

All checks pass. Standard processing.
Medium Risk
Yellow flags detected. Manager review required.
High Risk
Red flag detected. Ineligible for program.
📈
Post-Acceptance Monitoring
Continuous monitoring of participant behavior inside Rally
▾
  • Zoom IP cross-referencing: We compare the IP address used at signup with the IP used to join Zoom training events. Mismatches (e.g., US signup but international Zoom join) are flagged.
  • Contact entry speed & duplicates: We monitor for non-unique contacts in a participant’s network, and flag contact entry speeds under 30 seconds per contact.
  • Event attendance time: Participants averaging less than 20 minutes per training event are flagged as high risk. Under 40 minutes triggers a yellow flag.
  • Contact uniqueness threshold: Less than 70% unique contacts is a red flag. Between 70-80% is a yellow flag.
  • Ongoing fraud score: A separate BigQuery view assigns each active participant a rolling fraud score combining application-stage checks with in-program behavioral signals.
✅
Third-Party Validation
Independent verification that real conversations are happening
▾
  • Survey-based validation: In 2024, we surveyed people listed as contacts and compared them against a pseudo-control group. Treated respondents were 6.4 percentage points more likely to report that someone had talked to them about voting — confirming real, memorable outreach is happening.
  • 2026 field QC expansion: For Our Future's field apparatus will run regular QC SMS and phone campaigns among contacts to establish ongoing baselines and health metrics.

The infrastructure runs hourly. Four external APIs (IP geolocation, email domain reputation, AI text detection, and sanctions screening) are queried via Cloud Composer on a semi-hourly or hourly basis. Results are piped into BigQuery tables and surfaced through the App Vetting Dashboard in real time. Every application submitted since the last run is automatically checked.
